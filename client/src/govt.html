GOVERNMENT SCHEMES

Disability Pension

Financial assistance is provided through schemes like the Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS) to disabled individuals below the poverty line.

Educational Scholarships

Scholarships and fellowships for students with disabilities to support their education at different levels, including primary, secondary, and higher education.

Subsidized Loans

The National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) offers subsidized loans to disabled individuals for self-employment ventures.

Employment Reservation

Government jobs and public sector undertakings reserve a certain percentage of positions for persons with disabilities.

Skill Development Programs

Specialized training programs are provided to enhance the skills of disabled individuals to improve their employability.

Tax Benefits

Disabled individuals and their guardians can avail of tax deductions under various sections of the Income Tax Act.
