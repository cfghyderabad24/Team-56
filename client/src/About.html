Subhiksha Voluntary Organization Subiksha is one of the voluntary organisation, being registered as a non-government organisation (NGO), which has emerged with a group of like-minded individuals, on 8th Oct 2009, by a group of trained and qualified professional social workers, who are equally keen to bring their best towards attending to the children who require special care and need, especially when talking and mentioning about girl child. Mr. Pondi Kishore Kumar Founder Subiksha, has been particularly showing its attention to the special needs and disabilities such as: Intellectual Disabilities, Autism, Hearing Impaired, Mental Disabilities etc. These special needs are taken care of through proper therapies and further we give them proper education and advocacy.
